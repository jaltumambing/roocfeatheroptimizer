How stat weights work Each slider sets a multiplier (0.0–2.0) for that stat. The optimizer scores every build using:

Score = Σ (stat_value × weight)

0.0Ignore completely — copies spent here are wasted
0.5Nice to have, low priority
1.0Equal importance to other 1.0 stats
1.5Important — optimizer favors feathers that gain this
2.0Top priority — aggressively chased

⚠ Stats have different raw scales — IgnorePDEF reaches ~164 at Lv20 while PATK reaches ~56. Higher weight partly compensates for smaller raw values.
⚠ Only Raven's Hour set data loaded (4 Yellow + 1 Purple per statue). Surplus budget distributed globally by score-per-copy across all 10 statues.
