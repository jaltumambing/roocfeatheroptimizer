How stat priority works Assign a priority level to each stat. The optimizer scores builds using normalized stat values × your priority.

0Ignore — don't factor this stat
1Nice to have — low priority
2Important — optimizer favors this
3Priority — strongly chased
4Must maximize — top priority
⚠ Supports Raven's Hour (4Y+1P), Raven's Prestige (3Y+2P), and Raven's Plunder (2Y+3P) sets. Surplus budget distributed globally by score-per-copy across all 10 statues.

🧮 Inventory Calculator
How to use: Each column is a feather pool. Each row is a level. Enter how many feathers you own from that pool at that level — e.g. if you have 1 Sky at Lv8 and 2 Terra at Lv8, enter 3 in Sky/Terra · Lv8. The calculator adds up all copies consumed by your existing feathers. Hit Submit to fill Pool Inventory with those totals.
